about me

I am a professional intuitive Astrologer and Tarot Reader.

I can give insightful guidance to all areas of your life including love, career, family, healing, and your life path. I can also look into your past life/lives and see what karma you need to heal in this lifetime. We all have a past with our ancestry and I can give you ancestral guidance to break old patterns and generational curses that have been passed down.

In the wake of the pandemic, I decided to utilize my intuitive abilities and have been a professional Tarot Reader since 2020. I've always had a love for Astrology so I devoted my time to studying and accurately reading many different birth charts and past lives in 2023. I have over 140+ 5-star reviews from my lovely clients on Fiverr and Instagram. As well as this, I'm studying our connections beyond our solar system and am currently a Galactic Astrology student.

I'm here to give you guidance on this human journey. If you're looking for someone who doesn't sugar-coat, but has a soft touch, then I am the reader for you!
